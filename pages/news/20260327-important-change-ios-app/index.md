draft: true
date: 2026-04-06 18:00:00 +02:00
description:
---

For five years, the wallabag iOS application source code was maintained and
made available by @bourvill under the wallabag GitHub organization, and we
would like to express our gratitude for his dedication and contributions.

Lately the original maintainer has shifted focus to a closed-source version
(_version 7.6 onwards_) with its own set of paid features. After internal
discussion with the team, the core team and the maintainer decided to not
overseeing further development of the related open-source repository in the
foreseable future.

As a consequence the wallabag/ios-app repository will be archived but will
still remain available for anyone to take a look at the original source code.
Moreover this iOS application will stop being announced as the official
wallabag application for iOS platforms.

For any comment, inquiry or support request regarding this iOS application,
please refer to the related [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/wallabag-2-official/id1170800946) page.
